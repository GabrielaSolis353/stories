Second Dish: Miso Ramen

Miso ramen is a Japanese noodle soup originating from Hokkaido, Japan. This soup consists of a rich, thick, and savory broth made from fermented soybean paste (miso), and broth (pork or chicken). Toppings include ground pork, corn, butter, and bean sprouts. If you decide you do not want this dish, then please click here to take a look at our final dish.
